Party Planner takes your party planning experience to the next level! It allows you to schedule the theme, date of your party, your financial budget and everything you need to do to make it happen!

Party Planner gives you the opportunity to share a Party Mood Board and also attach pictures. This allows you to share with your friends how everyone felt about your party along with pictures to prove it!

Party Planner also features a shopping list for your party event. This allows the convenience of marking items off as purchased, subtracting the cost of those items from your remaining budget and to pinpoint the items still needed to get this party started!
